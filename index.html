WebCodecs

Direct access to hardware video decoders and encoders. No JavaScript decode loops. H.264, H.265/HEVC, VP9, and AV1 support depending on hardware. Frame-level access for precise editing.

WASM Engine

FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly for container demuxing, audio processing, and format conversion. Runs at near-native speed in a sandboxed environment. Zero server dependency for processing.

GPU Compositing

WebGL 2.0 compositing layer for real-time preview. Multiple video tracks, effects, titles, and transitions rendered on the GPU. Maintains 60fps even with complex timelines.
